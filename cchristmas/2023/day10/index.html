Celeste: Ultimate Selfie is a mod for Celeste Classic made in 4 days for the 12 Days of CChristmas Mod Jam. Featuring all new sprites, new mechanics, and everyone's favorite character, Theo! Venture into a deep cave, full of water and ancient mechanisms, what could lie at it's bottom? Theo definitely doesn't know, but he knows it'll make for the ultimate selfie.
