Stock Photo Stories!

Stock photo stories is an experiment in sequential visual storytelling. It's based off of the game Five Card Nancy, but tries to push it to a more abstract level. In this game, you build a 5 panel story by choosing from a collection of randomly selected stock photos. Is it too abstract to really be cohesive? Probably. I'd consider these comics as more of a starting point for your brain's imagination. After all, your brain projects the action between panels in any comic. This just takes it a little farther.

If you create something interesting, feel free to share it with me on Discord at RubyRed#8924

Made for UCLA Intro to Comics, Spring 2022
